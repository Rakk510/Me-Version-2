Modernity’s Legacy of Development Induced Displacement used deconstruction as a methodology to examine how the epistemological structures of Western Education infrastructure and its relation to displacement and the creation of refugee classes as a marginalized identity.

This talk was delivered at York University's annual Center for Refugee Studies conference as a part of "Neither Here Nor There: Refugees, Forced Migration, and the Landscapes of Displacement".
