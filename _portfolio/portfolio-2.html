A Monster! : Rediscovering Yourself" excerpt: "A storytelling and journaling game about reclaiming identity." collection: portfolio --- ## About the Game This is a storytelling and journaling game designed to explore identity and self-reclamation. It uses a “Yes, and…” and a “No, but…” system to navigate memories and key experiences. --- # The Game: Oh No! A Monster! You are you. (Yay! Go You! :3) One day you are just bopping along... when suddenly, **Oh No! A Monster!** ### What you’ll need: * **1 Pen/Pencil** and **Paper** * **1 Six-Sided Die (d6)** * **You** ### How to Play Think of something that makes you, you. As you rebuild yourself through key experiences, roll your d6: | Roll | Result | |:---:|:--- | | **1** | Yes, and... | | **2** | Yes | | **3** | Yes, but... | | **4** | No, but... | | **5** | No | | **6** | No, and... | ### Oh No! What am I!? (Prompts) * **Origins:** Did you know your biological parents? [cite_start]How close were you to those who raised you? [cite: 2] * **Geography:** Did you grow up where you are now? [cite_start]Or did you move here? [cite: 2] * **Values:** What is something you stand for? [cite_start]What is something you care about deeply?
