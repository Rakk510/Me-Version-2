This is an interactive story built in Twine. 
It is an autobiographical retelling of my first few moment after having a life saving surgery that left me phsyically and visually disabled. It is not the story of coming to terms with it, but rather trying to meet it in those first few moments before I was able to create a new mental idea of myself. It was the moment I had to meet myself and discover that who I used to be physically was not who I was anymore and reminding myself of the most important things to me and my life that made the sacrifice worth it.
