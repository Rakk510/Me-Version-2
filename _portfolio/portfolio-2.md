# Oh No! A Monster!
## Rediscovering Yourself

You are you. (Yay! Go You! :3)

One day you are just bopping along, being you in only the way that you can be you. When suddenly... 

**Oh No! A Monster!** AND IT'S RIGHT IN FRONT OF YOU. 

You’d do something… but you seem to have misplaced the bits of you that make you, you. You have to get them back! **AND FAST!**

---

### 🛠️ What you’ll need to remake you:
* **1 Pen/Pencil**
* **Paper**
* **1 Six-Sided Die (d6)**
* **You** ---

### 🎲 How to Play
This is a storytelling and journaling game. Each part of who and what you are should have a sentence or more behind it. Think of something that makes you (the character), you (the character). 

This game uses a **“Yes, and…”** and a **“No, but…”** system. As you rebuild yourself through key experiences and memories, roll your die:

| Roll | Result |
|:---:|:--- |
| **1** | Yes, and... |
| **2** | Yes |
| **3** | Yes, but... |
| **4** | No, but... |
| **5** | No |
| **6** | No, and... |

---

### 📝 Oh No! What am I!?
*Sample Prompts to get going…*

**Name & Identity**
* What is your Name and Age? 
* Are you Human? 

**History**
* Did you know your biological parents? How close were you to those who raised you? 
* Did you grow up where you are now? Or did you move here? 

**Relationships**
* Do you have friends? Allies? If so, who? If not, why? 
* How about enemies? Rivals? Loves?

**Inner Self**
* What is something you stand for? What is something you care about deeply? 
* Has anyone ever tried to hurt you through what matters to you?

> **The Big Question:** A person is more than their physical body; what is it that makes up you in the you-sized place that seems temporarily vacant?
